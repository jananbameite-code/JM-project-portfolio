1.1 Nametag

I downloaded the Prusa app and set it for the Prusa Mini+0.4mm nozzle. I downloaded the nametag 3MF file through a public workshop. (Operated the app from a mac) went to file → open project→ selected the 3MF file I used the scale function to enlarge the tag, and under the name portion I right clicked the text icon under the “keychain-blank.stl” (for the first and last name) and edited the text to say my name. From there I changed the font to the katari font. Using the mouse pad to click the name I was editing and adjusted its alignment to my liking. After editing the tag, I decided I wanted to do a color change. To that I clicked slice now and used the vertical toggle bar to animate the printing process by layer adding a “+” , by clicking the bar, to indicate in the g-code to the printer that a pause is to occur, where I will manually load new filament into the Prusa Mini at the layer where I want a color change to happen. Lastly, I saved the g-code file to the Usb drive, ejected it, and plugged it into the Prusa Mini and commenced the print.
